masthead
tokens components in use

A letterpress design system. Cool rag paper, blue‑black ink, twelve gemstone accents, hairline rules. The page is flat; only buttons lift off it; only the cancellation stamp is allowed to be crooked.

Everything the system draws is layout‑free. The .cl-* caller layer owns every margin, grid and measure on this page — proving the one rule: a component never carries its demo's spacing with it.

.stamp-button — a desk paperweight

default · -accent · -ghost · :disabled · -small · -largehover & press are live — try them

A matte solid lit from the upper-left: a key-light pool at that corner, the face curving into its own shade opposite, and a cast thrown down-right onto the paper. Hover brightens the light (no jump); pressing settles it in. The label is engraved. -ghost is the flat exception.
